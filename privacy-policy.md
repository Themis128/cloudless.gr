# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Cloudless.gr ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI development platform and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you use our services:

- **Account Information**: Email address, full name, username, and password
- **Profile Information**: Avatar URL, bio, location, website, and role
- **Contact Information**: Email address for communications and support
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials and session information

### 2.2 Usage Information
We automatically collect certain information about your use of our platform:

- **Session Data**: Login/logout times, session duration, and activity timestamps
- **Device Information**: IP address, user agent, device type, and browser information
- **Usage Analytics**: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns
- **Performance Data**: API request logs, error logs, and system performance metrics

### 2.3 AI Development Data
As an AI development platform, we collect:

- **Bot Configurations**: Bot names, prompts, models, and settings
- **Model Training Data**: Training parameters, datasets, and model configurations
- **Pipeline Configurations**: Pipeline steps, configurations, and execution logs
- **Project Data**: Project names, descriptions, and collaboration settings

### 2.4 Technical Information
We collect technical information to improve our services:

- **System Logs**: Error logs, performance metrics, and debugging information
- **API Usage**: Request patterns, response times, and usage statistics
- **Security Data**: Failed login attempts, security events, and audit logs
