# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025  
**Effective Date:** January 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Cloudless.gr ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website and use our AI development platform. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and more useful.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

**Purpose:**
- Maintain your login session and authentication state
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Enable core platform functionality

**Examples:**
- Session management cookies
- Authentication tokens
- Security verification cookies
- CSRF protection tokens

**Duration:** Session-based or up to 1 year

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

**Purpose:**
- Remember your language preferences
- Store user interface settings
- Maintain form data during multi-step processes
- Cache frequently accessed data for performance

**Examples:**
- Language preference cookies
- UI theme and layout preferences
- Form data storage cookies
- Feature toggle cookies

**Duration:** Up to 1 year

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform.

**Purpose:**
- Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
- Understand which features are most popular
- Identify areas for improvement
- Measure platform performance

**Examples:**
- Google Analytics cookies
- Performance monitoring cookies
- User behavior tracking cookies
- Error tracking cookies

**Duration:** Up to 2 years

### 3.4 Marketing Cookies
These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track marketing campaign effectiveness.

**Purpose:**
- Display personalized advertisements
- Track marketing campaign performance
- Measure conversion rates
- Retarget users with relevant content

**Examples:**
- Advertising platform cookies
- Social media tracking cookies
- Conversion tracking cookies
- Remarketing cookies

**Duration:** Up to 2 years

## 6. How to Manage Cookies

### 6.1 Browser Settings
You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:**
1. Go to Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred cookie settings
3. Block or allow specific cookies

**Firefox:**
1. Go to Options > Privacy & Security
2. Under "Cookies and Site Data," choose your settings
3. Manage exceptions for specific sites

**Safari:**
1. Go to Preferences > Privacy
2. Choose your cookie preferences
3. Manage website data

**Edge:**
1. Go to Settings > Cookies and site permissions
2. Choose your cookie settings
3. Block or allow specific cookies

### 6.2 Platform Cookie Controls
Within our platform, you can:

- **Manage Preferences**: Update your cookie preferences in your account settings
- **Opt-out of Analytics**: Disable analytics tracking in your privacy settings
- **Clear Data**: Remove stored data through your account dashboard

### 6.3 Third-Party Opt-outs
For third-party cookies:

- **Google Analytics**: Use the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on
- **Advertising Networks**: Use the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool
- **Social Media**: Manage preferences through respective platform settings

## 7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

### 7.1 Essential Cookies
Disabling essential cookies will:
- Prevent you from logging into your account
- Disable core platform functionality
- Cause security features to fail
- Result in session management issues

### 7.2 Functional Cookies
Disabling functional cookies may:
- Reset your preferences each time you visit
- Require you to re-enter form data
- Disable personalized features
- Reduce platform performance

### 7.3 Analytics Cookies
Disabling analytics cookies will:
- Prevent us from understanding platform usage
- Limit our ability to improve user experience
- Reduce our ability to identify and fix issues
- Not affect core platform functionality
